Market

GREEN PINDAYA COMPANY or TAUNG TAN NI tea production is constantly striving to produce qualified tea products. TAUNG TAN NI products are not just ordinary tea product, considering about the consumer health, creating good environment with no chemical for sustainable earth. Consumers also can be received standardize products. TAUNG TAN NI is participating with the organic tea products at local exhibition. Due to Covid 19 situation across the world and political situation of Myanmar, we have to postpone the export market. Currently we are producing for local retail and wholesale market. Now we are selling Organic Green Tea (Certified Product), Organic Pickled Tea (Certified Product), Natural Sundried Tea (Without Organic Certificate), Natural Green Tea (Without Organic Certificate) and Herbal Tea. Although main product of TAUNG TAN NI is tea, selling local products for our small farmers due to situating at the good natural resource hill station area.
