Networking

Collaboration
1 - Collaboration with the experience of Local and International Organization

Supported technical aided projects collaboration with the Helveats Myanmar, RBF, Control Union, CBI (Center for the Promotion of Import from Developing Country Netherland_ Capacity Building Training), ILO, Impact Hub for the development of PTCHT in Pindaya region. We are collaborating with the local organization such DOA, FDA, Department of Trade Promotion. The Danu ethnic female entrepreneur, Daw Nang Tharaphu Tint organized the tea farmers to participate in the project and trained farming technique for her local tea ethnic farmers as much as she can since 2005 to till now. Due to the providing of systematic farming method from these projects, farmers could produce standard Organic Green Tea and they received high price and increased their household income. But the opportunity is only for the nominated project area and not cover for the whole Pindaya region. However, TAUNG TAN NI will collaborate as much as we can for our region development.
